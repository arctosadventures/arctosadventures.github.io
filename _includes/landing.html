Explore nature while traveling.

Arctos is a community with a simple mission: to make outdoor experiences easily accessible for anyone. We believe that adventures in nature can be the best memories in your life – whether it’s sharing stories around the campfire, sleeping under the stars or topping a mountain.

But setting out for outdoor trips requires planning and the right kind of gear. Planning can be agonizing and high-quality outdoor gear is expensive. We take care of this part so that you can focus on the essential. Through us, you can book experiences, try and buy outdoor gear, and plan your own adventures.

HOW IT WORKS

Discover beautiful locations.

Find places for hiking, camping and outdoor experiences. Get all the essential information for planning your trip.

Book gear and experiences.

Try out extraordinary, field-tested outdoor gear. If you fall in love with the gear, you can buy it for less money.

Set out on an adventure.

The best part! Be an explorer and connect with local nature.
